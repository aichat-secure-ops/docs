### **What is Conversation Credits**

Generative AI works on the concept of tokens and our platform simplifies the concept through Conversation Credits.

1 conversation credit: a daily conversation between customer and chatbot.

### **Topping up when Low Credit Balance**

You can top-up your credits under **Billing.**

![Screenshot2025 03 27at2 56 39PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-03-27at2.56.39PM.png)
