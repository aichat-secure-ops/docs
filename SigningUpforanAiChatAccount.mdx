## How to Sign Up

Visit account creation page on [aichat.com](http://aichat.com) and click “Sign Up”.

You will be ask to submit your email address. Submit your email address for verification

![Submitemail Pn](/images/submitemail.png)

Click “Open Mail” or check your email inbox for email verification and verify your email address by clicking the verification link

![Openmail Pn](/images/openmail.png)

Upon successful verification, you will be asked for display name. At this stage you have successfully created an account.

![Displayname Pn](/images/displayname.png)
