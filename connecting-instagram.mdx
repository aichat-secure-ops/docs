Automate and engage with your Instagram audience.

## Integrating your Instagram account with AiChat allows you to:

- Monitor and respond to Instagram Direct Messages (DMs in a unified inbox)
- Provide 24/7 automated responses using chatbot flows
- Engage users through comment-to-DM automation
- Support live chat handoff to agents
- Track Instagram conversation performance

## 

## **How to Connect Your Facebook Page to the Chatbot**

#### Requirements:

Before connecting your Instagram account, ensure that:

1. **Use a Professional Account**
   - Your Instagram account must be a Business or Creator account. Personal accounts are not supported.
   - Learn how to [set up a professional account](https://help.instagram.com/502981923235522).
2. **Enable Messaging Access**
   - Ensure your Instagram messaging settings allow access to messages for connected apps.
   - Learn how to [manage access to Instagram messages across apps](https://help.instagram.com/585369912141614).
3. **Permissions**

You must be an administrator or have full access to the Instagram account you wish to connect.

![Instagram Guidelines (1)](/images/Instagram-Guidelines-\(1\).gif)

### **Step 1: Integrate Instagram Account in AiChat**

1. In your AiChat dashboard, go to **Settings → Channels → Instagram**.
2. Click **Connect Account** to start the integration process.
3. Sign in to your Instagram account through **Facebook (Meta)** when prompted.
4. Choose the Instagram account (linked to your Facebook Page) you want to connect.
5. After successful authorization, you will be redirected back to AiChat with the Instagram account connected.

![Instagram Connection (1)](/images/Instagram-Connection-\(1\).gif)

### Disconnect Instagram Integration

You can disconnect your Instagram account from our AiChat platform at anytime.

1. Go to **Settings → Channels → Instagram**
2. Scroll to **Disable Instagram Integration**
3. Click **Disable Integration**

Once disconnected:

- The chatbot will stop sending and receiving messages
- Automations and workflows will pause
