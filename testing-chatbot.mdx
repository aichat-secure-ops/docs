---
title: "Testing Your Chatbot"
description: "Test out your chatbot before you install on your website"
---

After you have updated your Knowledge Base, fine-tuned your chatbot personality or created flows, you can test them out in the Playground before you install on your website.

![Screenshot2025 03 28at11 38 39PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-03-28at11.38.39PM.png)

In Knowledge Base or Flows, find the button `Go to Playground` and you will brought to a separate Playground page where you can experience your chatbot.

![Screenshot2025 03 28at11 37 13PM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-03-28at11.37.13PM.png)
