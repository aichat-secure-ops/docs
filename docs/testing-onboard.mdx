## **How to Sign Up**

Visit the account creation page on [**aichat.com**](http://aichat.com) and click “Sign Up”.

Step 1: You will be asked to submit your email address. Submit your email address for verification.

![Onboarding1 Pn](/images/onboarding1.png)

Step 2: Click “Open Mail” or check your email inbox for email verification, and verify your email address by clicking the verification link.

![Openmail Pn](/images/openmail.png)

Upon successful verification, you will be asked for your display name. At this stage, you have successfully created an account.

![Displayname Pn](/images/displayname.png)

Once you’ve signed up, you’re ready to [Set up and onboard your bot →](/docs/testing-onboard1)
