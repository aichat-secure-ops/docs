To remove or edit a role, simply click the three-dot icon.

![Removeandeditrole Pn](/images/removeandeditrole.png)

After entering all the details, your business profile account will be fully set up and ready for use. This allows you to access all available features and functionalities within AiChat platform.

If you need to update or modify any information, simply navigate to the **Settings** option at the bottom right of the content dashboard.
